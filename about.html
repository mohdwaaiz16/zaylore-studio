ORIGINS

WHERE IT ALL STARTED

Every movement starts with a spark. As an emerging independent fashion brand and Bangalore fashion brand, ZAYLORE Studio was conceived with a clear and defiant purpose.

We believed that the modern generation needed a dedicated creator community and a premium clothing label in India that stood for raw ambition, individuality, and premium streetwear. We didn't set out to build another commercial clothing outlet; we set out to establish a definitive luxury apparel brand and designer fashion symbol.

Today, ZAYLORE Studio stands as a leading fashion brand in India and a high-impact streetwear brand in India. We unite creators, builders, artists, and motorcyclists under a shared aesthetic language. This is our story, and it is only the beginning.
