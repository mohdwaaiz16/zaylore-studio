The Visionary Behind It All

PM MOHAMMED WAAIZ

Founder & Creative Director · MW Zawion

With a singular mission to build India's most authentic premium streetwear and culture brand, PM Mohammed Waaiz channelled his passion for fashion, design, bikes, and music into building a movement from scratch. Zaylore Studio stands as the physical manifestation of this vision.

"Most people wear clothes to fit in. I built Zaylore for people who wear clothes to stand out. This is for the bold. The fearless. The ones who create culture, not consume it."

PM Mohammed Waaiz

As the founder of MW Zawion, a digital prestige studio crafting premium brand identities for next-generation businesses, Waaiz brings a dual force of fashion instinct and design intelligence to everything Zaylore represents.
