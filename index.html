ESTABLISHED IN INDIA

PREMIUM STREETWEAR BRAND IN INDIA

Welcome to the official universe of ZAYLORE Studio, the definitive luxury streetwear brand in India defining next-generation fashion, creator communities, and subcultures. As a rising Bangalore streetwear brand, we bridge the gap between premium global aesthetics and local culture. We specialize in producing elite collections of high quality streetwear and designer streetwear in India, built with heavier weights, bespoke cuts, and drop-shoulder silhouettes designed for dreamers, founders, creators, and riders who refuse the ordinary.

Explore our curated lineup of oversized streetwear designed to make a statement. ZAYLORE Studio offers the highest quality oversized t-shirts in India, featuring custom fits, high-density prints, and drop-shoulder silhouettes. Our collection expands into contemporary streetwear essentials like premium hoodies, baggy denim, and functional cargo trousers that reflect the pulse of modern streetwear clothing. Each garment stands as a testament to our status as a premier luxury fashion brand in India, serving those who seek more than just apparel.

From our headquarters in Bangalore, we are actively constructing a thriving streetwear culture in India. Our design house focuses on luxury oversized clothing and premium apparel in India that merges street aesthetics with luxury tailoring. By aligning with innovators, motorcyclists, artists, and entrepreneurs, ZAYLORE Studio is more than an independent clothing label—it is an aesthetic movement. Browse our collections today and experience the pinnacle of streetwear clothing in India.
